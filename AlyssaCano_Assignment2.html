Top 3 National Parks I want to visit!

By: Alyssa Cano

Map 1:

National Park #1: Prince of Wales Island, The Tongass National Forest, Alaska, USA

The Tongass National Forest is a temperate rainforest and is the largest forest in the United States. Prince of Wales Island is a part of the Tongass National Forest and has a beautiful landscape filled with mountains, streams, and 2 million acres of forest. I would like to go on a weekend or four day backpacking trip through this stunning area.

Map 2:

Glacier National Park, Montana, USA

Glacier National Park in Montana is known for the various glacier formations. Unfortunately these glaciers are slowly disappearing due to climate change. I would like to visit this park to see the remaining glaciers and to hike through the mountains in the area. This area is continually changing due to climate change and seeing these drastic changes will be interesting.

Map 3:

National Park #3: Carlsbad Caverns National Park, New Mexico, USA

The Carlsbad Cavern has various underground chambers. The total extent of the rooms and passages continues to be unknown. The explored part of the main cavern is more than 30 miles long, 3 miles of this stretch is open to visitors. Inside the caverns there are large stalagmite, stalactites, and other cave deposits. I am mainly attracted to this location due to these types of cave structures and deposits, as this area creates an otherworldly feeling.
